Lady Blackbird

Lady Blackbird is a free, rules-light RPG and setting by John Harper. There is a mechanic in Lady Blackbird where you are given a target number and a set of dice, and you have to decide before rolling whether you wish to add additional dice from your personal pool. I am always wondering how much value I get from adding additional dice, so I wrote a calculator for it.
